## SEO basics (what actually matters)

### 1. Title tags (most important)
- Your article title IS the title tag
- Put the keyword at the START of the title
- Keep it under 60 characters (Google cuts off longer ones)
- ✅ "Best Noise Cancelling Headphones 2025"
- ❌ "My Thoughts on Some Headphones I Tried Recently"

### 2. Meta description
- The excerpt field in your articles
- 150-160 characters
- Include the keyword + a reason to click
- I already optimized these for you

### 3. Content structure (H2, H3 headings)
- Use `##` for section headings (already done in your articles)
- Include keywords in headings naturally
- Google uses headings to understand what your page is about

### 4. Word count
- Google prefers in-depth content (1,000+ words)
- Your Medium articles are 900-1100 words — good
- Your in-app articles are shorter (300-500 words) — these are fine for the site, but the Medium articles are what will rank

### 5. Internal linking
- Link between your articles (e.g., in the headphones article, link to the mouse article)
- This helps Google understand your site structure
- I can add this if you want

**Action:** Publish all 3 articles in `download/medium-articles/` on Medium.com. This is the #1 thing you can do for SEO.

### 2. Get backlinks (other sites linking to you)

Google ranks sites that other sites link to. Ways to get backlinks:
- **Medium articles** → link back to your site (in the author bio or naturally in the article)
- **Reddit posts** → link to your articles (but follow Reddit rules — see the Reddit strategy guide)
- **Pinterest pins** → each pin links to your site
- **Quora answers** → answer questions about headphones/mice/etc. and link to your reviews
- **Guest posts** → write for other blogs in exchange for a link back

### 3. Be patient

SEO takes 3-6 months to start working. Google needs to:
1. Crawl your site (1-2 weeks)
2. Index your pages (1-4 weeks)
3. Test your content in search results (1-3 months)
4. Trust your domain (3-6 months)

Do not expect Google traffic in week 1. Do expect it by month 4 if you keep publishing.

## Free SEO tools to use

### 1. Google Search Console (FREE — essential)
- Go to **search.google.com/search-console**
- Add your Vercel URL
- Google will show you:
  - What searches bring people to your site
  - What pages are indexed
  - Any errors to fix
- This is the #1 tool for understanding your Google traffic

### 2. Google Analytics (FREE)
- Go to **analytics.google.com**
- Shows you where your visitors come from
- Which pages they visit
- How long they stay

### 3. Ubersuggest (FREE tier)
- Go to **ubersuggest.com**
- Type in a keyword → see search volume + related keywords
- Use this to find new article topics

### 4. Answer the Public (FREE)
- Go to **answerthepublic.com**
- Type in a keyword → see what questions people ask about it
- Write articles answering those questions
