## 💡 POSTING TIPS

1. **Post 2-3 times per week minimum** — consistency beats volume
2. **Always include a photo or video** — posts with images get 3x engagement
3. **Use the link every time** → https://side-hustle-stack-beta.vercel.app/
4. **Post seasonal content 2-3 weeks BEFORE the holiday** — people book in advance
5. **Engage with comments** — reply to every comment within 24 hours
6. **Share customer photos** (with permission) — social proof is powerful
7. **Mix it up** — don't only post sales. Post behind-the-scenes, tips, and personality too.
