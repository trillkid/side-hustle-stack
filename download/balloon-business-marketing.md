## 💡 Pro tips to help her book more

1. **Photos sell this service** — the more photos of her work she can post, the more bookings she'll get. People want to SEE what they're getting.

2. **Weekends are prime time** — encourage people to book early. "Saturdays in [month] are filling up!" creates urgency.

3. **Offer package deals** — "Garland + 6 balloon bouquets for $300" sounds like a deal and increases average order value.

4. **Ask for referrals** — every happy customer knows 3 other people planning parties. "If you loved your decor, I'd be so grateful if you told a friend!"

5. **Get on Google** — eventually, create a free Google Business Profile for her. That's how people searching "balloon decor Toronto" will find her. (I can help with this later.)
