## How to publish each one on Medium

1. Go to **medium.com** → sign in → click **"Write"** (top right)
2. Open the `.md` file in any text editor (Notepad, TextEdit, VS Code)
3. Copy all the text
4. Paste it into Medium's editor — Medium auto-formats markdown (headings, bold, etc.)
5. **Before publishing**, replace `YOUR_AFFILIATE_LINK_HERE` with your real Amazon affiliate link for that product (see below)
6. Add a cover image (use a product photo from Amazon, or an Unsplash image)
7. Click **"Publish"** and add the tags listed at the top of each article

## How to get your Amazon affiliate links

Once your Amazon Associates account is approved:

1. Go to **affiliate-program.amazon.com** → sign in
2. Click **"Product Linking" → "Product Links"**
3. Search for the product (e.g. "Sony WH-1000XM5")
4. Click **"Get Link"** → copy the short URL
5. Paste that URL in place of `YOUR_AFFILIATE_LINK_HERE` in the article

Do this for each article. That's it — when someone clicks your link and buys (anything on Amazon within 24 hours), you earn a commission.

## Tips for getting traffic to your articles

- **Share on social media** (Twitter/X, Reddit, LinkedIn) with a hook
- **Write consistently** — Medium's algorithm favors accounts that publish regularly
- **Engage with other writers** — clap and comment on similar reviews
- **Submit to Medium publications** (like "The Startup" or "Better Marketing") for more reach
- **Each article takes ~30 min to publish** — do all 3 in one sitting

## Important: Amazon's 180-day rule

Amazon requires you to make **at least 1 qualifying sale within 180 days** of signing up, or they close your account. Publishing these 3 articles and sharing them gives you a real shot at that first sale. Don't sign up and then do nothing — get the articles live within the first week.
