# Google Business Profile Setup Guide
## Get found when people search "balloon decor Toronto"

A Google Business Profile is FREE and is the #1 way local customers will find your mom's balloon business. When someone in Toronto searches "balloon garland near me" or "balloon decor Toronto," Google shows local businesses at the TOP of results — above regular websites. This is huge.

---

## Step 1: Go to Google Business Profile

1. Go to **business.google.com**
2. Sign in with a Google account (Gmail). If your mom doesn't have one, create a free Gmail like `torontoballoondecor@gmail.com` — use this for all business stuff.
3. Click **"Manage now"** or **"Add your business"**

## Step 2: Enter the business name

**Business name:** Use something descriptive + searchable. Good options:
- `Toronto Balloon Decor` *(best — includes the keyword + city)*
- `[Mom's Name] Balloon Decor Toronto`
- `Balloon Garlands Toronto`
- `Party Balloons Toronto`

⚠️ **Don't use a generic name** like "Jane's Balloons" — include "Toronto" and "Balloon Decor" so Google matches local searches.

## Step 3: Choose the business category

**Primary category:** `Balloon artist` (this is an actual Google category — pick it!)

If you don't see "Balloon artist," search for it. It's a real category. Alternative options:
- `Party planner`
- `Event planner`
- `Balloon store`

"Balloon artist" is the best one — it's the most specific.

## Step 4: Set the service area

**Service area:** Toronto + surrounding areas. Add these cities/regions:
- Toronto
- North York
- Scarborough
- Etobicoke
- Mississauga
- Brampton
- Markham
- Vaughan
- Richmond Hill
- Pickering

**Important:** You can choose "I don't have a physical storefront" — select service area instead. This is correct for a balloon decor business that goes to customers' venues.

## Step 5: Add contact info

- **Phone number:** Your mom's phone number (this shows on Google — make sure it's one she answers)
- **Website:** `https://side-hustle-stack-beta.vercel.app/` (your site — people can book from here)

## Step 6: Add photos (THIS IS CRITICAL)

Photos are the #1 factor in whether people contact you from Google. Add:

1. **Logo / profile photo** — can be a nice photo of balloon work
2. **Cover photo** — a wide shot of a beautiful balloon garland
3. **At least 10 photos of balloon work** — garlands, arches, bouquets at real events
4. **Photos showing different categories** — birthday, wedding, holiday, etc.

**Photo tips:**
- Use real photos of your mom's work (phone photos are fine if well-lit)
- Take photos in good lighting (natural daylight is best)
- Show the garland IN the event setting (not just close-ups)
- Get wide shots that show the full installation
- Add new photos every few weeks — Google ranks active profiles higher

## Step 7: Write a business description

**Copy and paste this:**

```
Custom balloon decor for every celebration in Toronto & the GTA. We create beautiful balloon garlands, arches, columns, and bouquets for birthdays, weddings, baby showers, corporate events, and holiday parties.

Every installation is custom-designed to match your theme and colors. Air-filled garlands last 3-5 days. We deliver and set up across Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area.

Packages start at $65 for balloon bouquets and $250 for a 10-foot garland. Wedding and seasonal packages available.

Book online at our website or call to discuss your event. We respond within 24 hours.
```

## Step 9: Set business hours

Set hours that make sense for when you accept calls/bookings. For example:
- Monday-Friday: 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM
- Saturday: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Sunday: Closed (or 12:00 PM - 4:00 PM)

**Events happen on weekends** — but you can note "Events by appointment" in the hours notes.

## Step 10: Verify the business (IMPORTANT)

Google will ask you to verify the business. Options:
- **Phone call** — fastest, Google calls with a code
- **Text message** — Google texts a code
- **Video** — you record a short video showing your business (for service-area businesses)

Choose phone or text if available. Enter the code Google gives you.

Once verified, your profile goes live on Google Maps and search results — usually within 24-48 hours.

---

## After verification: 5 things to do EVERY WEEK

### 1. Add 2-3 new photos every week
Every time your mom does an installation, post photos. Google ranks active profiles higher, and new photos keep the profile fresh.

### 2. Respond to every review
When customers leave reviews (ask them to!), respond personally:
- "Thank you so much, [Name]! It was a pleasure creating your garland. 🎈"
- Responding shows Google (and future customers) you're active and engaged.

### 3. Post Google Updates (like mini social posts)
Google Business Profile has a "Posts" feature. Post weekly:
- New packages
- Seasonal promos ("Book your Halloween garland now!")
- Behind-the-scenes photos
- Customer testimonials

### 4. Ask every happy customer for a review
Reviews are the #2 ranking factor (after proximity). After every event, text the customer:
> "Hi [Name]! So glad you loved the balloons 🎈 If you have 2 minutes, a Google review would mean the world to us: [your review link]"

Get your review link at: Google Business Profile → "Read reviews" → "Get more reviews" → copy the link.

### 5. Answer questions in the Q&A section
Google has a Q&A section. Pre-populate common questions yourself:
- "Do you deliver to [city]?" → "Yes! We serve Toronto and the entire GTA."
- "How far in advance should I book?" → "We recommend 2-4 weeks for weekends, but we sometimes have last-minute availability."
- "Do you do custom colors?" → "Absolutely! Every garland is custom-designed to match your theme."
